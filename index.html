About

This holiday season, in lieu of paper greeting cards and empty sentiments, we at Propane Studio have chosen to make a difference by focusing our energies on those less fortunate. With that goal in mind, we’ve partnered with the National Fuel Funds Network (NFFN), a non-profit dedicated to organizing programs for heating assistance across the country.

To get us all in the spirit of giving, we’ve started spreading the warmth with a donation of $1,000! With the pilot light now lit—we need your help in bringing heat to those who can’t afford it this holiday season! Any donation, big or small, will go directly to the NFFN and bring joy to those in need.

Ready to help? Give here.

Other Ways to Give

If you’d like to also spread holiday cheer to other wonderful do-good organizations, below is a list of charities we encourage you to consider:

American Red Cross

The Red Cross: the leading humanitarian organization providing disaster relief.

Mazon

Mazon: a Jewish-led organization working to end hunger among people of all faiths in the U.S.

Feeding America

Feeding America: a mission to fight hunger through a network of food banks.

Kiva

Kiva: an international micro-loan agency giving entrepreneurs funds to become self-sustaining.

The Salvation Army

The Salvation Army: an internationally-renowned charity for providing social aid.

One Warm Coat

One Warm Coat: giving warmth by collecting used and new coats for those in need.

Toys For Tots

Toys for Tots: an organization dedicated to collecting and distributing toys to children.

ModestNeeds.Org

Modest Needs: promotes self-sufficiency in low-income workers by providing help with short-term emergency expenses.
